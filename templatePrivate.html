Notes

If you read this, it means this report have been interesting. Thank you for having read everything :)
Even if the data are a direct interpretation of your logs, do not take that for an absolute truth.
Here are points that might alter these information (and notes):

  • Only data from Messenger are processed. So, other interaction (SMS, phone calls, meetings, etc) are not included here.
  • You can merge data from other backups. But if the name of the conversation changed, you may have moments without data.
  • One data can have different meanings. Keep a critical point of view on the results.
  • If you do something bad with these results, I won't be held responsible.
  • Other functionalities will be added. Don't forget to check regularly my GitHub for bugfixes.
